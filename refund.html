Refund Policy

This Refund Policy explains the conditions under which refunds may be granted for purchases made on NeoMindzLive.

Last updated: January 2026

1. Digital Products

NeoMindzLive provides access to a digital IQ test. Due to the nature of digital products, all sales are considered final once access to the test has been granted.

2. When Refunds May Be Granted

Refunds may be considered only in the following cases:

  • Technical issues: If a technical problem on our platform prevents access to the test and the issue cannot be resolved within a reasonable time.
  • Duplicate payments: If you were charged more than once for the same test.

3. Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will not be issued in the following situations:

  • Dissatisfaction with test results or score.
  • Failure to complete the test after access was provided.
  • Misunderstanding of the product description or features.
  • User errors or unsupported devices/browsers.

4. Refund Request Process

To request a refund, please contact our support team at neomindzlive@gmail.com and include:

  • Proof of purchase (transaction ID or receipt).
  • A brief description of the issue.
  • Screenshots, if applicable.

Our team will review your request and respond within 3–5 business days.

5. Payment Processor

Payments are processed by Paddle. Approved refunds will be issued back to the original payment method according to Paddle’s processing timelines.

6. Contact

If you have questions regarding this Refund Policy, please contact us at neomindzlive@gmail.com.
